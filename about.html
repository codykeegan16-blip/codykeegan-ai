About the Lab

I don't have a secret system.

This isn't a course. This isn't a guaranteed path to anything. CodyKeegan.AI is a public workbench where I test, break, and stack AI tools to see what happens.

The goal is simple: leverage. Can we connect X to Y to build automated commerce machines? Can a one-person operation punch with the weight of twenty? That's what I'm trying to find out.

Everything shared here is an experiment. Some of it works beautifully. Some of it crashes and burns. Take the ideas, use the tools, but know that you're operating in the frontier. Your results are entirely your own.
