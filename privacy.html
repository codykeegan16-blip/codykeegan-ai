Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Your privacy is important. Here is how CodyKeegan.AI handles your data when you visit the site or enter the lab.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up to get access to the dashboard, we collect your email address. This is strictly so we can send you the tools, links, and updates about the experiments we're running. We do not collect names, payment info, or physical addresses through the site.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your email address to operate our email list, managed via Kit (formerly ConvertKit). We don't sell, rent, or trade your data to third-party brokers. We send you emails about AI tools, automation experiments, and commerce.

3. Third-Party Services

To operate the lab, we use the following third-party services that may process limited data on our behalf:

  • Kit — email list management and form submissions
  • Cloudflare — site hosting and basic traffic analytics
  • Clearbit — logo images displayed on the dashboard

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

4. Cookies and Tracking

This site may use basic cookies for traffic analysis and to see what experiments people are looking at. Affiliate links may contain tracking parameters used by third-party affiliate programs. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you want to fly under the radar.

5. Third-Party Links

The dashboard is full of links to third-party AI tools. Some of these are affiliate links (marked with an asterisk). Once you leave CodyKeegan.AI, you are subject to the privacy policies of those specific tools. We don't control what they do with your data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to the mailing list. If you unsubscribe, your email is removed from active lists. Kit may retain anonymized or aggregated data in accordance with their own retention policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request we correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request we delete your data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out — unsubscribe from emails at any time

These rights apply to residents of the EU/EEA (under GDPR), California (under CCPA/CPRA), and other jurisdictions with similar data protection laws. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at terminal@codykeegan.ai.

8. Unsubscribing

You can opt out of the email list at any time. Every email sent from the lab has an "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom. Click it, and you're off the list instantly. No questions asked.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach out at terminal@codykeegan.ai.
