Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Your privacy is important. Here is how CodyKeegan.AI handles your data when you visit the site or enter the lab.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up to get access to the dashboard, we collect your email address. This is strictly so we can send you the tools, links, and updates about the experiments we're running.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your email address to operate our email list (managed via ConvertKit). We don't sell your data to sketchy third-party brokers. We just send you emails about AI and commerce.

3. Cookies and Tracking

This site may use basic cookies to analyze traffic and see what experiments people are looking at. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you want to fly under the radar.

4. Third-Party Links

The dashboard is full of links to third-party AI tools. Once you leave CodyKeegan.AI, you are subject to the privacy policies of those specific tools. We don't control what they do with your data.

5. Unsubscribing

You can opt out of the email list at any time. Every email sent from the lab has an "Unsubscribe" button at the bottom. Click it, and you're off the list instantly.
